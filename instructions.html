Instructions for using this app

  • First, go to toolbar and click on "Add on", click the name of the current document , then click "Run"
  • You will enter your personal infomation for your resume using the 5 buttons(Header,Experience,Skills,Education,Footer) in the sidebar, close the pop up windows after you enter information for each button.
  • click on the button to have pop up windows to enter information about experience, education, resume header information and skillsets.
  • Go back to the sidebar and choose resume template or the section you want to include. Notice that all templates in "Templates" will include all information of 5 sections
  • If you want to display only a subset of 5 sections, a specific format of resume will be used. i.e. if you choose only subset of sections you can't choose the template format, but if you include all info, you can.
  • ** Click Survey and complete a survey to help us to improve our program! A graph will be generated.
  • ** Click Close to close the sidebar and exit the program



Functions, GUI to be implemented

  • showExperienceDialog(),showFooterDialog(), showSkillSetDialog(), showHeaderInputDialog(), showEducationDialog() will be called in "script" tag of the corresponding html file to have pop up windows
  • generateTemaplate1(), generateTemaplate2(), generateTemplate3() will put the basic structure on the google doc
  • "putXXXInfo()" (such as putExperienceInfo(), putEducationInfo(), putHeaderInfo(), putFooterInfo(), putSkillsetInfo() will put the corresponding info to target location
  • GenerateSections() will set up the document structure and only include the sections the user selected
