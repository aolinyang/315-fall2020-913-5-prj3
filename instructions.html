Instructions for using this app

First, go to toolbar and click on "Add on", click "test_for_table_6_cells" , then click "Run"

You will see a menu with buttons and drop down menu. Choose the resume template and text color you want to use, click the corresponding buttons to submit your choices

click on the button to have pop up windows to enter information about experience, education, resume header information and skillsets.




Functions, GUI to be implemented

The onOpen() function creates a option for users to click in the toolbar of google doc "Add on" button. The showSidebar() function display the sidebar.html and display it as sidebar in the doc.

showHeaderInputDialog() will let headerInput.html pop up and showSkillSetDialog() will let skillSets.html pop up. So does showExperienceDialog().

The storeExperienceInfo() will be modify and handle backend process(inserting information into google doc / storing information into an array in Code.gs.
